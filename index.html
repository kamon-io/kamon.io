Tailing the logs is not enough

Everybody starts monitoring with logs because they are there by default. Just connect to your server and start tailing.

But logs have a hard time showing you the overall response times for your application, or whether certain calls to the database are happening in sequence or parallel (among a million other things).

The solution is not to ditch logging, though. You get the best troubleshooting powers by complementing your logs with application-level metrics and traces, and that's what we help you achieve.
