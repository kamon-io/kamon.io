The Three Jobs of Every Observability Platform

On this webinar we will discuss instrumentation, monitoring, and debugging: the three jobs that every observability platform must help you get done

On this Webinar

On this webinar we will depart from the buzzwords and marketing around observability to focus on the three jobs that your observability platform must help you get done:

  • Instrument your applications to produce telemetry data
  • Monitor your system's vitals so that you know when something goes wrong
  • Dig through telemetry data to debug any problem that might come your way

We will also touch on the most common telemetry data types (logs, metrics, and traces), how each one of them is better suited for monitoring or debugging tasks, and the common gotchas you must take into account when building your observability platform.
