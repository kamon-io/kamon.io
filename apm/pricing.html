Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay for Kamon Telemetry?

Nope. Kamon Telemtry is and will always be free and open source.

What are you charging for?

The pricing plans apply only to Kamon APM. You can monitor up to 5 microservices for free with Kamon APM.

If your needs grow out of our Free plan you can upgrade to the Grow plan. The cost can be boiled down to €1 per service per day.

How do you charge?

At the end of the month we add €1 for each day a service was active, and €1 for each extra million spans. That's it.

What if I need a custom plan?

We can accommodate more metrics, spans and retention for you. Contact us via email at hello@kamon.io.

What languages/frameworks does Kamon support?

What is the overhead of monitoring services with Kamon?

The typical performance impact of Kamon is between 1% and 3%. If you notice anything above that, please contact our support and we will help you optimize it.

How do test environments work?

Test environments have limited retention of 24 hours and can be used for monitoring staging, development and pre-production environments services, absolutely free of charge. Only included in the Grow plan.

How often will you charge me?

We charge at the beginning of every month.

Will you charge if my services are down?

No. You will only be charged for the days your services sent data to Kamon APM.

What type of support is available?

We offer community support through Github Discussions, live chat in Kamon APM, and you can always request a one-on-one session.

How do I start using Kamon?

Click on the "Start Monitoring" links and follow the instructions. It should take you 3 minutes to get started.
